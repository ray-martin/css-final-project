CALL FOR ARTISTS

Mosaic Life Care Juried Exhibition: Mosaic Life Care and the Albrecht-Kemper Museum of Art invite artists who reside within 100 miles of St. Joseph, MO, to participate in the Mosaic Life Care Juried Exhibition. This year, the theme is Artist’s Choice. Each artist may submit an unlimited number of works for the $20 entry fee. Deadline for all submissions is Friday, August 6, 2021 at 11:59 PM.
